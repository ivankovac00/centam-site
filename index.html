Who We Are

A dedicated team of experts in advanced ceramics and Si-SiC systems

CENTAM is a materials science and engineering company founded by PhD researchers with deep expertise in advanced ceramics, with a strong focus on Si-SiC systems. We bridge the gap between cutting-edge academic research and scalable industrial application.

Our work covers material characterization, additive manufacturing and post-processing development, quality analysis, and technical consulting. We partner with manufacturers, research institutions, and technology companies to solve complex challenges in advanced materials.

What We Strive For

We believe that superior materials enable superior products. Our mission is to translate advanced materials science into practical industrial value, delivering rigorous analysis, expert insight, and engineered solutions that support real-world performance and reliability.

SCIENTIFIC RIGOR

Every recommendation is grounded in evidence and peer-reviewed methodology.

INDUSTRIAL RELEVANCE

We focus on solutions that work in real production environments.

LONG-TERM PARTNERSHIP

We invest in understanding our clients' challenges deeply.

What We Do

Engineering advanced carbide ceramics for industrial use

Our products and services are divided in six classes covering the full material production, optimization and characterization.

Material Characterization

Comprehensive analysis of ceramic materials — microstructure, phase composition, mechanical properties, and thermal behavior.

Custom Ceramic Development

From formulation to firing: we design and develop ceramic components tailored to your performance specifications and production requirements.

Failure Analysis & Material Diagnostics

In-depth studies including failure analysis, microstructural evaluation, and performance assessment of ceramic components.

Process Engineering

Improve your ceramic manufacturing processes — reducing waste, improving consistency, and enabling scalable production.

R&D Consulting

Expert support for research and development projects. We integrate with your team to accelerate materials innovation timelines.

Quality Control & Testing

Independent testing and quality verification services for ceramic components, aligned with international standards.

The Team

PhD level expertise, built for application

CENTAM is led by researchers who built their careers in advanced ceramics manufacturing and now bring that depth to industry partners.

Dr. Berfu Göksel

Managing Director & Co-founder

Doctor of Materials Engineering (KU Leuven). Berfu Göksel is Managing Director and Co-founder of CENTAM, leading strategy, business development, and industrial collaborations.

Dr. Sebastian Meyers

Director of Operations & Co-founder

Doctor in Mechanical Engineering (KU Leuven). Specialised in powder bed fusion additive manufacturing and post-processing of Si-SiC, various metals and polymers. Complete process chain optimisation, from powder to final part.

(Dr.) Mia Kovač

Director of Process & Product Development & Co-founder

(Doctor) of Materials Engineering (KU Leuven). Specialised in ceramic additive manufacturing and full process optimization, from powder to final ceramic product. Connects material formulation, processing, characterization, and application requirements to support product development.

Dr. Margherita Beretta

Director of R&D & Co-founder

Doctor of Materials Engineering (KU Leuven & Ghent University). Specialised in post-processing of metal and ceramic 3D printed components. Her work focuses on understanding and tuning the process-structure-property correlation of Si-SiC components.

Waut Declercq

Technical Expert & Co-founder

Doctoral researcher at of Materials and Mechanical Engineering Departments at KU Leuven. Specialised in powder bed fusion additive manufacturing and post-processing of Si-SiC, Waut supports design, material development and process optimization of Si-SiC components.
